Mid South Home Buyers offer a variety of financing options for both U.S. and international investors.
For our American investors, we’re able to offer top notch mortgage brokers who specialize in investment property. For our international buyers, we have bank financing available with competitive rates.
CONVENTIONAL FINANCING
We update our website weekly with the newest rates available from our preferred lenders for a 25% down investment, though down payments as low as 20% down are available for slightly higher rates.

Investment loans are available through our lenders with approved credit, terms as follows:
 - 20% of Purchase Price Down
- 30 Year Amortization
- Fixed Rate
- Closing costs will include title work, title insurance, loan costs and escrows for taxes and insurance
ALTERNATIVE FINANCING
Invest With Your Retirement Fund
This financing is available to U.S. and non U.S. citizens and entities such as LLC’s. It is a non-recourse loan, so it may be used to finance purchases from self-directed IRAs or Roth IRAs. There is no underwriting, and can be utilized by buyers for whom FHA loans are not an option.
⦿ 40% Down
⦿ 7.9% Interest Rate
⦿ 12-Year Amortization
⦿ No Loan Minimum
⦿ Available for Any Purchase Price
Closing costs for both options include:
⦿Title Work
⦿Title Insurance
⦿ Loan Costs
⦿ Taxes and Insurance Escrows
