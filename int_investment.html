INTERNATIONAL INVESTORS
Our team will help you select, purchase, and profit from your property.
All from the comfort of your own home!
Investing in property from the other side of the world can be a daunting task, however as our many international investors already know, the stars are aligned for wealth accumulation through U.S. investment acquisitions. Real estate prices are at an all time low in the U.S. and rental demand has never been higher.
Mid South Home Buyers is set up for you to select, purchase, and profit from your investment all from the comfort of your own home. However, we love it when our buyers visit us. If you are considering purchasing, feel free to come visit one of the most entertaining cities in America and view our operations for yourself. We reimburse our buyers $500 for travel expenses upon their first purchase.
Mid South Home Buyers specializes in making overseas transactions seamless and hassle free. Just a few of the resources we offer to international investors are:
Bank Financing
In-house property management
Closing Attorneys/Solicitors who specialize in long distance settlements
Assistance in setting up LLCs
Top notch title work guaranteeing your investment is free of liens
HD videos of your property if it falls vacant
Detailed reports sent to you monthly
Rents disbursed in the currency of your choice, no U.S. bank account required
Direct deposit of your rents
Availability of our staff to you before, during, and after the sale
