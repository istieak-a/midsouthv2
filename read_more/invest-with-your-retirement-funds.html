Invest with your retirement funds.
More and more, investors across the U.S. and around the world are tapping their retirement funds to invest in cash flow-producing rental property. In the United States, investors utilize their IRAs by converting their accounts to self-directed IRA custodians. Equity Trust IRA​, although completely unaffiliated with Mid South Home Buyers, has an astounding reputation for providing details and ease of transaction regarding conversion of accounts and purchase of property. We highly recommend you take a close look at the returns you're currently receiving in your retirement accounts and give serious consideration to self-directed retirement options.
